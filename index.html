About Us

We are making a metal mold for cars, and our metal molds are essential for car makers like TOYOTA, MITSUBISHI and so on.

We have been providing a lot of metal molds for several types of cars since we had established in 1969. Our 50th anniversary will come next year, that means we are proud for having a long history. There are 2 factories in Japan, Nagoya city(the headquarter) and Osaka city. We also have some small factories and partners in Asian countries like the Philippines and Vietnam.

Our strong point, comparing to other companies, is a great experience, skill, and capital investment. Through our long history, We could get a better experience than other companies. And the experience improved our skill. And now, We have good original curriculums to teach an advanced skill to our employees. And we have been investing our machinery to update and improve our quality and productivity. Last year, We have had the latest factory built in Osaka city. Our client, car makers, expect our more contribution.

Our Culture

Our company culture is honest and faithful. Sometimes We go out for dinner together after work. And we have some club activities like baseball, football and so on. Most staff belong one or more clubs. Such an activity make the interpersonal relationships at the workplace better.
